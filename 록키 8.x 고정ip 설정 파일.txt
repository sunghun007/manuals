록키 8.x 고정ip 설정 파일

/etc/sysconfig/network-scripts/

고정ip 예

TYPE=Ethernet
BOOTPROTO=none
NAME=eno1
DEVICE=eno1
ONBOOT=yes

IPADDR=192.168.150.131
PREFIX=24
GATEWAY=192.168.150.1
DNS1=8.8.8.8
DNS2=1.1.1.1

dhcp 예

TYPE=Ethernet
BOOTPROTO=dhcp
NAME=eno1
DEVICE=eno1
ONBOOT=yes
